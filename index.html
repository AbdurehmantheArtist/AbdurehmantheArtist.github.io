About the Artist & Developer

Abdurehman is a multifaceted creator with over 5 years of experience in traditional art and a growing passion for coding. His artwork combines traditional techniques with modern sensibilities, while his coding projects showcase practical and creative applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Specializing in colored, graphite, and mixed media installations, alongside web development projects, Abdurehman brings a unique perspective to both art and technology.
